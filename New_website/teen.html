Texas teen licensing, explained

Getting a Texas license at 16 has a few required steps.

Here's the simple version — and we help with every one.

01

Get the learner permit

Complete the classroom portion and pass the written knowledge test to earn the learner license.

02

Behind-the-wheel training

In-car lessons with a certified instructor, plus supervised practice hours logged with a parent.

03

ITTD course

The short Impact Texas Teen Drivers video, required before the road test.

04

Pass the DPS road test

Which you can take right here with us — no long wait at the DPS office.

A note on requirements

Verify exact Texas hour requirements (classroom, behind-the-wheel, observation) and ITTD/permit rules against current TDLR/DPS guidance before enrolling — specifics change.
